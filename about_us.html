LiftBuddy



Who we are

Introducing LiftBuddy, your go-to carpooling platform during extreme weather conditions.

Our app is specifically designed to help commuters get to work or school safely and conveniently, by connecting them with fellow employees or students at their workplace or school.

With LiftBuddy, you can rest easy knowing that you have a reliable and secure means of transportation, even in the most challenging weather conditions.

Connecting us together

LiftBuddy aims to bring communities together during extreme weather conditions. The app operates solely on donations and allows passengers to tip drivers for their services. The main objective of LiftBuddy is to facilitate mutual aid among community members during difficult times.
